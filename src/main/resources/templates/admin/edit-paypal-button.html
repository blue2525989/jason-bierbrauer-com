Edit PayPal Buttons

Create new PayPal button


Login to your PayPal account, from the top menu select the Tools drop-down menu and then click Business Setup. This will redirect you to a page asking how you want to setup PayPal on your website, choose "Option B: Add HTML buttons to your website yourself" and then click Create payment buttons.

From this point you have the option of making custom buttons for your items. Read through the options and select what you need and then click Create Button at the bottom of the page. Simply copy and paste the generated HTML code into the text area and your finished!
